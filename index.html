about

Hi, my name is EJ Sorrento

I am a 22 year old web developer from Buffalo, New York.

Following my graduation from high school in June 2012, a friend and I started a business together focused on bringing nutritional and other products to our local community. During that time, I came across quite a few programmers and when it was time to finally move on from that company, I knew the direction I wanted to go in next.

I have spent the last 12 weeks in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program building many different apps and perfecting my craft as a developer. Outside of that, I focus on other skills I find essential to coding, currently I'm focusing on broadening my understanding of Vanilla JavaScript.
